01

DEFINITIONS

1.1
"MCK" refers to Micro Cement King and its authorised representatives. "Client" refers to the party named in the associated quotation. "Works" refers to all microcement application, surface preparation, sealing, and related services described in the quotation scope.
1.2
"Contract" means the accepted quotation together with these Payment Terms & Conditions, forming the entire agreement between the parties.
02

QUOTE VALIDITY & ACCEPTANCE

2.1
Quotes are valid for the period stated on the quotation from the date of issue. After expiry, pricing must be reconfirmed in writing by MCK.
2.2
Acceptance of the quotation is confirmed by the client's signature (digital or physical) on the quotation document AND receipt of the booking deposit. Both conditions must be met.
2.3
MCK reserves the right to withdraw or amend any quotation prior to formal acceptance.
03

PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 — Booking Deposit
A non-refundable booking deposit is required to secure the project start date. Deposit is 5% of contract value for contracts exceeding $20,000, or 10% for contracts under $20,000.
3.2 — Material Payment
50% of the remaining contract value (after deposit) is due as a material payment prior to the scheduled start date. Works will not commence until this payment is received as cleared funds.
3.3 — Final Claim
The remaining balance is due within 3 business days of practical completion and client sign-off. Practical completion is defined as the point at which the works are substantially complete and fit for intended use.
3.4 — Progress Claims
For contracts exceeding the credit limit ($10,000), MCK reserves the right to issue progress claims at reasonable intervals. Progress claims are payable within 3 business days of issue.
3.5 — Upfront Payment Reduction
A 5% reduction (capped at $1,000) is available for clients who pay the full contract amount upfront prior to commencement. This reduction is applied to the total contract value (ex GST).
3.6 — On-Site Measure Fee
A non-refundable on-site measure fee of $220 (ex GST) applies where a site visit is required prior to quoting. This fee is credited in full against the contract upon acceptance.
04

OVERDUE PAYMENTS & DEBT RECOVERY

4.1
Invoices overdue by more than 3 business days will incur an administration fee of $220 (ex GST).
4.2
Interest accrues at 3% per week on outstanding amounts from Day 4 of the overdue period, compounding weekly.
4.3
If payment remains outstanding for 30 days or more, the matter will be referred to an external debt recovery agent. All recovery costs, legal fees, and collection charges will be added to the outstanding amount and are payable by the client.
4.4
MCK reserves the right to suspend or terminate works immediately if any payment milestone is not met within the specified timeframe.
05

VARIATIONS

5.1
All variations to the original scope must be agreed in writing before work commences. Verbal agreements are not binding.
5.2
Variations are charged at $150/hr (ex GST) with a 2-hour minimum, plus materials at cost + 20%. A $500 material allowance applies per variation unless otherwise agreed.
5.3
Variation invoices are payable within 3 business days of issue and are subject to the same overdue terms as the primary contract.
06

WARRANTIES

6.1
All workmanship is covered under statutory warranties as required by Queensland law.
6.2
Manufacturer product warranties are passed through to the client in full. MCK will assist with any warranty claims where reasonable.
6.3
Warranties do not cover damage caused by misuse, neglect, other trades, structural movement, or failure to follow maintenance guidelines provided by MCK.
07

DISPUTE RESOLUTION

7.1
No third-party contractors may be engaged to rectify alleged defects until a written resolution is agreed upon by both parties. Engaging third parties without prior written consent voids all warranties.
7.2
Any disputes shall first be addressed through direct negotiation. If unresolved within 14 days, the matter may be referred to mediation in accordance with Queensland law.
08

SITE ACCESS & CONDITIONS

8.1
The client must ensure unobstructed access to the work area for the full project duration. Restricted access, delays caused by other trades, or site conditions not disclosed at quoting may incur additional charges.
8.2
MCK is not responsible for damage to surfaces caused by other trades working in or near the application area after MCK has completed its works.
09

COLOUR & FINISH DISCLAIMER

9.1
Microcement colour and finish may vary from samples due to substrate conditions, ambient lighting, humidity, and application technique. Samples are indicative only and do not guarantee an exact colour match.
9.2
Colour changes requested after application has commenced will be treated as a variation and charged accordingly.
10

TERMINATION

10.1
MCK reserves the right to terminate the contract if payment terms are repeatedly breached or if site conditions present an unreasonable risk to workers or materials.
10.2
Upon termination, all work completed to date is invoiced at the agreed rates and is immediately payable. Materials ordered or delivered are non-refundable.
11

GOVERNING LAW

11.1
This agreement is governed by the laws of Queensland, Australia. Any legal proceedings shall be conducted in the courts of Queensland.
